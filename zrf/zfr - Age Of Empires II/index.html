About Game

In this game you can play different Campaigns, Random Maps, Regicide, Death Match and can also Save your game.

Campaigns: There are 5 different Campaigns (missions), in the Campaign section you will need to collect wood, food, gold and stone through your villagers. After that you can make different fighters such as Swords Men, Cavalery, Seig units, war ships by spending these wood, food etc. At last you will attack on your target or in some missions you will defend your allies.

Random Map: In trail version there is only one map (coastle) and only on tribe (Celts) for you but in this game there are about 7 tribes, you can select either of them. Also in trail version there are only 2 computer players but in this version you can select the computer players from 1 - 13. With addition you can also change Difficulty level, Map Size, Map Type, Resources amount, Population limit (25 - 200), Reveal Map, Starting Age (Dark age - Post Imperial age) and Victory type.

Regicide: Regicide is similar to Random Map but in this section you will have a king, if your king is killed, you are defeated.

Death Match: In Death Match you and your oppositions will have alot of resources from the start, you will just create army and fight WAR with your oppositions. It is also similar to Random Map in settings like you can change the scenario by your own choice.

Saved Game: In this section you will get the games you have saved whether in Campaigns, Random Map, Regicide, or Death Match.

At the end of the game or by quitting the game, you will see a dash board which will show you the result and time line of the game you played.
